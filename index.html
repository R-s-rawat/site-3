No sugar, No oil. All real food.

We are Northern Ohio's first oil-free and sugar-free granola company.

We believe in not only having delicious foods but nutrious foods that you can feel good about eating.

Our granola is made with best ingredients we can find and make with love in our own home in beautiful Shaker Heights.

Being sugar free, we use whole dates as our sweetner of choice. None of that processed sugar in any of its forms here! Please take a look at our available products to find out more about best dishes from us.

Some of our products

Maple Pecan Granola

A delightful blend of sweet maple syrup and crunchy pecans, offering a balanced and flavorful granola experience.

Ingredients: Oats, Pecans, Maple syrup, Coconut oil, Vanilla extract, Salt, Cinnamon, Dried cranberries.

Chocolate Almond Granola

A classic and crowd-pleasing combination of rich chocolate chips and nutty almonds for a satisfying treat.

Ingredients: Bananas, oats, millet, buckwheat, dried choco, chia seeds, coconut milk, water, orgainic guar gum.

Cinnamon Walnut Granola

Warm, comforting flavors of cinnamon and raisins combined with the crunch of walnuts create a cozy granola option.

Ingredients: Oats, Walnuts, Raisins, Cinnamon, Honey or maple syrup, Coconut oil, Vanilla extract, Salt.
